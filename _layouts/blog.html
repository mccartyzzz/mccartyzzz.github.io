Why Blog

1. This will help us improve our ability to express ourselves and learn how to express a thing clearly.

2. Everyone has a forgetting habit, and blogging helps us keep track of what we've learned.

3. We can also find more like-minded people by sharing our knowledge.

I didn't blog before, so maybe that's why I forgot what I learned. From now on, I will try to write a blog to share the difficulties I have encountered in my work and study and the knowledge I feel is important. It is very important. So if you like my blog, thank you very much, let's make progress together!
